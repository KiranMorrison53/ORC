Fair Pricing

The price of an Orc at mint is 1 SOL. There are no price tiers and our Orcs are priced at a level to ensure ORC is as accessible as possible.

To ensure everyone gets a chance to mint an Orc, you will be limited to 1 Orc per transaction. However there is no overall limit per wallet and you can purchase multiple Orcs. O.R.C members with multiple Orcs will receive a role based on our tiered rank structure and will have larger voting rights in our holder voting system. Your Orc metadata will be permanently stored on arweave.

Our goal is to build a community - a virtual members club where like-minded collectors from all over the world can hangout and contribute to building a project on Solana.

The Specs

Each Orc is a unique and algorithmically generated collectible using base layers hand drawn by our artists.

There are over 250+ attributes across 11 categories: background, shoulder, body, clothes, necklace , markings, mouth, tusk, eyes, bling, head. These attributes determine how rare an Orc is, with each trait ranging from common to ultra rare.

Every single Orc still has membership to the Orc Racing Club and will still be able to access member-only perks, such as using your Orc in our upcoming video game and contributing to community-led decisions.
